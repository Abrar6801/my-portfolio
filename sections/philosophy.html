Research Philosophy

01

I start with the data

Every system I build begins with exploratory analysis — understanding distributions, finding anomalies, and letting the data reveal its structure before writing a single model. Whether it is 14,000 e-commerce records or aerial jet imagery, the data always has something to say first.

02

I ship explainable systems

Black-box ML has limited production value. In AIReviewHub I built a confidence scoring and reasoning trace layer so developers could trust and verify every suggestion. Explainability is not a feature — it is the foundation of adoption.

03

I bridge research and product

My IEEE-published CNN research did not stop at 94% accuracy on paper. I applied the same transfer learning and preprocessing principles to build deployable, optimized systems. Research only matters when it runs in production.
